Key Story Arcs

Every island. Every battle. Every tear.

East Blue Saga

The first saga introduces Monkey D. Luffy and his journey to form the Straw Hat Pirates. Key arcs include Romance Dawn, Orange Town, Syrup Village, Baratie, and Arlong Park.

Alabasta Saga

The Straw Hats venture into the Grand Line and help Princess Vivi save the Kingdom of Alabasta from Crocodile and the Baroque Works organization.

Skypiea Saga

A journey to the sky island of Skypiea to uncover ancient secrets and defeat the self-proclaimed god Enel.

Water 7 Saga

The Straw Hats confront CP9, an elite group of assassins, to save Nico Robin and acquire their new ship, the Thousand Sunny.

Marineford Saga

A climactic saga where Luffy tries to save his brother Ace, leading to the epic Marineford War that changed the world forever.
